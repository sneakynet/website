Who is SneakyNet?

SneakyNet is a Local Exchange Company specializing in Vintage Computer Festivals, providing services that are period correct for a late 1990s context.

Does SneakyNet Use VOIP?

No! Our entire network uses Time Division Multiplexing (TDM) to provide guaranteed latency and throughput. Each customer circuit is a fully dedicated 64kbit/s channel.

How is my Data Processed?

Your data is processed in a manner which enables maximum revenue for the phone company.

Is My Telephone Private?

To ensure your privacy, we do not monitor lines for content, availability, or even usefulness. The SneakyNet Security Team recommends checking for people standing behind you before discussing sensitive information.
